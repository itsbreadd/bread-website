<h2>Projects</h2>

Due to the fast-paced environment of ecommerce I have been exposed to a variety of languages and frameworks; giving me the opportunity to contribute an array of projects. I pride myself on writing readable and maintainable code, ensuring every file i touch is left in a readable, maintable state, and is up to PSR12 standards.

Heres a short list of things I have worked on during my time at Paul Smith:

  • > Centra

    Led a full scale eCommerce platform shift from Magento 2 to Centra, playing a pivotal role in creating our Laravel-based middleware. Refined and crafted critical business logic, developed complex mapping tables/views, and flawlessly integrated Centra through GraphQL & REST. Took full ownership of product creation/updates (ECOM & B2B) from ERP, designing intricate mapping logic, orchestrating B2B customer imports/exports, and streamlining ERP-to-Centra data flows.
    The frontend was created on an ACL-controlled Filament 3 interface, empowering key teams to effortlessly manage mapping tables, monitor reporting, and perform custom actions like importing/exporting data, campaign management, and coupon handling. Behind the scenes, I optimized operations with Upstash (our Redis cache), ensuring critical data was cached and ready for quick access on the frontend, fueling performance and scalability.

  • > Nosto

    Implemented a pre-built Magento 2 module, applying necessary patches and improvements to enhance its functionality. Managed recommendations and merchandising rules, developing Velocity templates to support and optimize frontend visuals.

  • > Klevu

    Led the replacement of Nosto with one of my favorite third-party implementations — Klevu (soon to be Athos Commerce) — taking full ownership of its implementation and management. Engineered an event-driven custom catalog sync using XML, REST APIs, and webhooks, ensuring a seamless/accurate data flow. Developed a custom product update queuing system from Centra/Storyblok -> middleware -> Klevu, efficiently linking essential product data like images and mappings. By eliminating internal cache reliance, leveraging Klevu’s own caching system through their storefront API's, I optimized performance and reduced costs on serverless function usage. This significantly enhanced product listing pages, search, and recommendations.

  • > Google Tag Manager (GTM)

    Solely managed the implementation of Google Tag Manager (GTM), integrating tracking codes across multiple platforms (Magento 2, Headless, Centra) and various programming languages. Configured data layer pushes and manipulated data to ensure accurate event tracking for services such as Klaviyo, GA4, Rakuten, Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter, Google Ads, and more.

  • > Server Side Google Tag Manager (SGTM)

    To enhance tracking accuracy, I implemented a server-side Google Tag Manager (sGTM) contained proxied through a subdomain to send events to various third parties, including Facebook Conversion API, Pinterest Conversion API, and GA4. Hosting the sGTM server in a first-party environment helped mitigate script blocking and improved data reliability. Additionally, I developed custom templates, including a caching logic for loading specific scripts from sGTM, optimizing frontend response times.

  • > Cookie Consent

    Designed, implemented, and managed numerous cookie banners (User Centrics, Cookie Script, Etc) across multiple platform transitions (Magento 2, Headless, Centra), ensuring seamless integration with custom styling. Utilized Google’s Consent Mode in GTM to dynamically control tag firing based on regional user consent.

  • > Antavo

    Led the backend development and strategic planning of Paul Smith’s loyalty project, driving key architectural decisions and leading high-impact meetings. Engineered a scalable, event-driven system leveraging Azure Logic Apps, Function Apps, Service Buses, and Event Hubs over in-house middleware to maximize performance and reliability. Designed and implemented webhook-based data flows, meticulously mapped out with flow diagrams in Miro, to seamlessly update customer data: points balances, orders, returns, and more.
